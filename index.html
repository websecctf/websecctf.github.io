About Us

WebSec CTF happens everyear and is focused on various challenges. We utlize multiple opensource design for our backend to make sure we can provide a user applicable CTF. Our challenges will vary from Web security to Reconnaisance. Main goal of the WebSec team is to help reseachers expand their scope and attack a network in a unique manner.

Writeups of our CTFs are provided right after they go offline. These writeup will provide in detail on how a challenge could be solved. Links to the writeups will be made available once the CTF is over.

Preparing

Our challenges vary on difficulty. To best prepare will be to be active in the community and learn about new exploits and vulnerabilties.

Along with it, our new CTF will include multiple OSes which needs to be secured. For this, it is best that a competitior has experience on the Operating Systems we provide.
