This CTF is conducted for Nepali Reserachers. In this CTF, you will be provided with multiple challenges each differing in different methods and types. Challenges range from web to networking. You will have reversing challenges as well as many others. In this individual competition, you will compete against other hackers from Nepal for a 24 hr competition.

The goal of this CTF, is to help introduce Nepali hackers into new, feasible ways of hacking. You will learn about tools (hopefully) and ways to fasten your vulnerability research process.

  • CTF runs for 24 hrs from July 7, 2017 to July 8, 2017.
  • You must be residing in Nepal to be eligible for prizes.
  • Its individual competition not a team event.

What is WebSec CTF?

WebSec CTF is an intiaitve launched by group of security researchers, working to provide CTFs to hackers from multiple countries multiple times through out the country. We run our CTFs 2-3 times a year for countries or worldwide.

If you want to be part of this group working to increase security expertise around the world contact us at ctf@securityredteam.com
